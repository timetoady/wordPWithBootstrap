History

In March of 1996 we purchased 10 restaurants from the company we worked for. These included full service and family style as well as a quick service restaurant. In addition we we took our smaller catering business and began to grow it. Throughout the years we have continued to grow as a specialized caterer in Utah, Idaho, Wyoming and even Nevada.

Mission

We take care of all your event needs allowing you to fully enjoy your special day. We love realizing your vision and exceeding your expectations, and our many talented staff have the ability to do just that. Our company motto is and always will be “we do the work so you don’t have to.”

Purpose

Caley’s Catering & Events specializes in the planning and catering of weddings, for both large and small groups, but we also cater other events such as company parties, training camps for athletes, family get togethers, fundraisers, and birthdays. From casual buffet style to formal white linen service, our goal is to do the work so you can enjoy your party or event to the fullest.
