About Dream Vacations LLC®

From the beginning, it was never about making money. The money would come. It always does, when excellence leads the way.

When Charles Allen JR. envisioned a company that would focus on experiences rather than just destinations, memories more than events, he pulled together ever wonderful experience he'd carefully noted down in his numereous travel scrapbooks over the course of 30 years, and set down to quantify exactly what made them special.

The company kept was important: no doubt. The difference between having the people around that brought him joy, and having them accomidated, was all the difference. From there, a thousand small places and finds, a million good feelings and reflections, from the touches of the accomidations, to the look of the mountains and the skies, to the taste of the water.

Finally, the piece of mind. That the time was well spent, that money wasn't the solution for everything, and that rest and satisfaction were at the end. All these pooled together to build even beyond what you dream a vacation could be.
